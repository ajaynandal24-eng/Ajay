# Ajay — Reflective Learning Portfolio (MOD004453)

This repository contains the template and structure for the 3,000-word Reflective Learning Portfolio for the Leadership and Change Management module.

## Contents

- **[portfolio-template.md](portfolio-template.md)** — Structured template with all three components, required evidence checklists, suggested word counts, and key theories/frameworks to reference.

## Portfolio Components

| Component | Weight | Suggested Length | Topic |
|-----------|--------|-----------------|-------|
| 1 | 20% | ~600 words | MBTI Result and Leadership Style |
| 2 | 30% | ~900 words | Belbin Role and Teamwork Reflection |
| 3 | 50% | ~1,500 words | Change Management Plan (AI Marking at ARU) |

## Getting Started

1. Complete the [MBTI Test](https://dynomight.net/mbti) and save your result
2. Complete the [Belbin Team Roles Test](https://www.123test.com/team-roles-test/) and note your preferred role
3. Use `portfolio-template.md` as a guide to structure your writing
4. Replace the placeholder guidance with your own reflective analysis

## Note

The actual portfolio content must be written individually based on your own test results, SLW experiences, and reflective analysis. The template provides structure and guidance only.
